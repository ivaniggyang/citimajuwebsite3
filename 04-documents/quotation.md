# Quotation Guide

## Purpose

A quotation is a formal, commercial document. It must be clear, complete, and professional — it is often the first formal document a client receives from Citi Maju and sets the tone for the entire project relationship.

---

## When to Issue a Quotation

Issue a quotation when:
- A client has made a specific scope inquiry and you have enough information to price it
- You have completed a site survey and are ready to submit pricing
- A client requests pricing in writing

Do not issue a quotation based on incomplete scope. If you do not have enough information, request a site visit or scope clarification before quoting.

---

## Quotation Reference Numbering

All quotations must carry a unique reference number.

**Format:** `[Entity]-Q-[YYYY]-[XXX]`

Examples:
- `CME-Q-2025-041` (Citi Maju Engineering, 2025, 41st quotation)
- `CMC-Q-2025-012` (Citi Maju Construction)

Log all issued quotation numbers to avoid duplication.

---

## Required Fields

Every quotation must include:

1. **Header** — entity letterhead (Engineering or Construction, whichever is the contracting party)
2. **Reference number**
3. **Date of issue**
4. **Validity period** — standard: 30 days unless otherwise stated
5. **Client details** — company name, attention to, address
6. **Project description** — specific, not vague
7. **Scope of works** — itemised list of what is included
8. **Bill of quantities / pricing table** — itemised, with unit rates where applicable
9. **Exclusions** — what is explicitly not included
10. **Terms and conditions** — payment terms, variation order procedure, assumptions
11. **Signatory block** — name, title, signature, date

---

## Layout and Typography

Use the entity letterhead as the base document.

**Section headings:** Sora 600, 10pt, Text Dark
**Table headers:** Sora 600, 8pt, ALL CAPS, Background Blue Pale (#E8F1FB)
**Table body:** Noto Sans 400, 9pt, alternating White and Cream (#F7F4EF) rows
**Total row:** Noto Sans 600 or 700, Brand Blue (#1B4F8A) background, White text
**Gold accent:** 1px gold hairline below the document title and above the total row

## Scope of Works — How to Write It

Be specific. Vague scope leads to disputes.

**Good:**
```
Supply and installation of 200mm HDPE pipe, Class PN12.5, from MH01 to MH07 (approx. 320m), including:
- Pipe jacking by horizontal directional drilling
- Thrust block construction at entry and exit pits
- Hydrostatic pressure testing
- Reinstatement of surface to original condition
```

**Bad:**
```
Supply and install pipes as per discussion.
```

List inclusions as bullet points. List exclusions separately and clearly.

---

## Exclusions — Always State These

Standard exclusions to include unless specifically quoted for:
- Soil disposal / contaminated material surcharge
- Diversion of existing utilities
- Works outside the agreed boundary / chainage
- Night work or weekend work surcharge
- Variations to scope arising from unforeseen subsurface conditions

| | | | | GST / SST | — |
| | | | | **Total** | **XX,XXX.00** |

State clearly whether prices are inclusive or exclusive of applicable tax.

---

## Payment Terms (Standard)

Unless agreed otherwise:
- **Progress billing:** Monthly, based on work completed
- **Payment terms:** 30 days from invoice date
- **Retention:** 5% held until defects liability period expires (12 months from practical completion)

State these terms explicitly in every quotation.

---

## Validity

Standard validity is **30 days** from the date of issue. State this clearly:

> "This quotation is valid for 30 days from the date of issue. Prices are subject to revision upon expiry."

If material prices are volatile, reduce validity to 14 days and state the reason.

---

## Signatory

Only a Director or authorised signatory may sign quotations issued on behalf of Citi Maju. Do not issue an unsigned quotation.
