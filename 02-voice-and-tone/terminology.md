# Terminology Reference

## Company Names — Approved Forms

| Context | Use |
|---|---|
| General reference (all materials) | Citi Maju Group |
| Legal documents, quotations, contracts | Citi Maju Engineering Sdn Bhd or Citi Maju Construction Sdn Bhd (whichever is the contracting entity) |
| Short form in running text | Citi Maju (acceptable after first full mention) |

**Never use:**
- "CME" or "CMC" as standalone abbreviations in external materials
- "the company" when the full name should be used
- "our two companies" or any phrasing that implies two separate brands

## Certifications — Approved Terms

Always state certifications exactly as below. Do not abbreviate or rephrase.

| Certification | Correct Form |

Note: These certifications apply to **Citi Maju Engineering Sdn Bhd** only. Do not list them on Construction entity materials.

## Industry Terms — Usage Notes

**Trenchless:** Acceptable as a descriptor — "trenchless pipe installation" — but always follow with the specific method (pipe jacking or directional drilling) when precision matters.

**Hot tap / hot tapping:** Either form is acceptable. Preferred form: "hot tapping."

**G7:** Always include "CIDB" before it. "CIDB G7" not just "G7."

**Reticulation:** "Water reticulation" is the correct industry term. Do not simplify to "piping" or "pipeline work" in formal materials.
