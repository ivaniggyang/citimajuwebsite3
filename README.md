## About Citi Maju Group

Citi Maju Group is a Klang Valley construction and engineering group comprising two registered entities, always presented as one unified group.

**Citi Maju Engineering Sdn Bhd** handles large-scale water reticulation projects, serving major property developers, Air Selangor, and government procurement. Certified: CIDB G7 (Malaysia's highest contractor grade), SPAN Registered, Air Selangor Approved Contractor.

**Citi Maju Construction Sdn Bhd** handles renovation works, pipe jacking and directional drilling, hot tapping, and smaller water reticulation works.

**Tagline:** Trusted. Certified. Delivered.
**Contact:** inquiry@citimaju.com | www.citimaju.com

## Non-Negotiable Rules

These apply to every single material, without exception:

1. **Always "Klang Valley"** — never "KL and Selangor"
2. **Always one group** — never present as two separate companies
3. **Gold is #C8921A** — never use #E8A020 or any other amber
4. **H1 and H2 headings use Cormorant Garamond** — never Sora or Noto Sans
5. **Tagline is fixed** — "Trusted. Certified. Delivered." No variations
6. **No machinery rental** — do not list it as a service
7. **No response time promises** — do not state turnaround times for inquiries

---

## Visual Benchmark

The design benchmark for all Citi Maju materials is Goldman Sachs corporate design: restrained, institutional, and authoritative. Precision over decoration. Navy and gold as the dominant combination. Nothing should look like it was made quickly or cheaply.
