# Uniforms

## Philosophy

Uniforms signal that this is an organised, professional operation. A site team in consistent, clean, branded workwear makes a different impression than a mixed crew in personal clothing. All Citi Maju personnel on site or in client-facing roles must wear the company uniform.

---

## Uniform Types

| Role | Uniform |
|---|---|
| Site supervisors and engineers | Branded polo shirt, long work trousers, safety boots |
| Site workers | Branded work shirt or safety vest over personal clothing |
| Office / admin | Branded polo shirt or dress shirt (business casual) |
| Director / management (on-site visit) | Branded polo shirt (minimum) with full PPE over |

---

## Polo Shirt — Standard (Site and Office)

### Specifications

**Base colour:** Navy (#071E3D) or Dark Navy (closest fabric match — specify Pantone 289 C or closest)

**Fabric:** 200gsm cotton-polyester blend, moisture-wicking for site use

**Embroidery (left chest):**
- CM Mark (white thread) + entity name below in Sora 300 ALL CAPS (white thread)
- Height of embroidered mark: 40–50mm
- Position: left chest, 80mm from shoulder seam, 60mm from placket edge

**Back print or embroidery (optional):**
- "CITI MAJU GROUP" across the upper back
- Sora 700, ALL CAPS, white, 20–25mm letter height
- Or: CM Mark only, centred on upper back, 80mm height

### Sizing
Order in a range: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, XXXL. Confirm sizing chart with your supplier — sizes vary significantly between brands.

---

## Safety Vest

For site workers who require high-visibility coverage over their clothing:

**Type:** ANSI/ISEA Class 2 (or equivalent SIRIM-approved) high-visibility vest
**Colour:** Orange or lime-yellow (follow site or client safety requirements)
**Print:** "CITI MAJU" across the back in reflective print or screen print, Black text
**Size:** XL-XXXL to fit over clothing

The safety vest is a safety garment first. Follow the PPE requirements of the site and client. Branding is secondary to compliance.

---

## Work Trousers

- Colour: Dark Navy or Charcoal (no logo needed)
- Fabric: 240gsm cotton or cotton-poly, reinforced knee panels for site
- Pockets: Multiple cargo pockets acceptable for site use

---

## PPE Over Uniforms

All site personnel wear standard PPE over the uniform:
- Safety helmet (see helmet-badge.md for helmet and badge specifications)
- Safety boots (steel-toed, EN ISO 20345 or equivalent)
- Safety glasses or goggles as required by site
- Gloves as required
- High-visibility vest as required

The uniform is the layer under PPE. It must still be neat and clean — visible at site entrances and during client visits.

---

## Condition and Presentation

- Uniforms must be clean and pressed when worn to a client site or in an office setting
- Torn, stained, or faded shirts must be replaced
- Shirts must be tucked in or worn neatly — not hanging open or unbuttoned
- No personal modifications: no rolling sleeves up past the elbow in client-facing situations, no writing on uniforms

---

## Ordering

Work with an approved embroidery or workwear vendor. Provide:
- Embroidery design file: CM Mark at the specified dimensions, entity text below (vector AI or SVG)
- Thread colour: White (closest DMC or Madeira thread colour for #FFFFFF on navy fabric)
- Font specification: Sora 300 ALL CAPS for entity name (provide as a vector path, not live text, to the embroiderer)

Minimum order: 10 units is typically the minimum for custom embroidery. Order in batches per site team.

File naming for embroidery artwork:
```
CMG-Embroidery-[Entity]-Chest-[YYYY].ai
CMG-Embroidery-[Entity]-Back-[YYYY].ai
```
