# Helmet Badge

## Purpose

The helmet badge identifies Citi Maju personnel on site. It must be legible at a distance, durable under site conditions, and clearly branded.

---

## Badge Design

### Shape and Dimensions
- **Shape:** Rectangular with rounded corners (preferred) or die-cut shield shape
- **Size:** 60 × 40mm (standard) or 80 × 50mm (for larger helmets)

### Front Face

**Background:** Deep Navy (#071E3D)

**Logo:** CM Mark (white variant), centred horizontally, positioned in the upper two-thirds of the badge

**Entity or Group line:** Sora 300, ALL CAPS, 5–6pt in design file (approximately 4–5mm on badge), White, centred below the mark

**Certification line (Engineering personnel only):**
`CIDB G7` in Sora 600, ALL CAPS, 5pt, Gold (#C8921A), centred below entity line

**Optional border:** 1px gold (#C8921A) border, 2mm inset from the badge edge

---

## Colour Coding by Entity

To distinguish Engineering and Construction personnel on a shared site:

| Entity | Helmet Colour | Badge |
|---|---|---|
| Engineering | White helmet | Navy badge, "CITI MAJU ENGINEERING" |
| Construction | White helmet | Navy badge, "CITI MAJU CONSTRUCTION" |
| Director / Management | White helmet | Navy badge, "CITI MAJU GROUP" |
| Subcontractor (on site) | Yellow helmet | No Citi Maju badge |

Helmet colour coding follows Malaysian OSH standard requirements. White is reserved for supervisors and management. Yellow for general workers. Red or orange for safety officers depending on site convention.

---

## Material and Durability

The badge must withstand:
- Direct sun (UV resistance)
- Rain and humidity
- Adhesive must not peel at site temperatures (up to 50°C on a metal surface in Malaysian sun)

**Recommended materials:**
- 1.5–2mm PVC substrate with UV-laminated print
- Or: Anodised aluminium with screen print
- Adhesive backing: high-tack automotive-grade adhesive, or rivet mount for long-term use

**Not acceptable:**
- Standard paper sticker
- Office laser-printed label
- Generic name badge holder attached to the helmet

---

## Application

Badges are applied to the front of the helmet. Position: centred horizontally, upper third of the front panel.

Clean the helmet surface with isopropyl alcohol before applying adhesive badges. Press firmly for 30 seconds. Allow 24 hours before exposing to water or extreme heat.

For riveted badges: use a professional badge mounting tool. Do not pierce the helmet shell in a way that compromises structural integrity — consult the helmet manufacturer's specification for approved mounting points.

---

## Ordering

Minimum order is typically 20 units from most badge vendors. Order in batches when onboarding new site teams.

Provide the vendor with:
- Design file in PDF or AI format
- Colour specifications: Navy #071E3D, Gold #C8921A, White #FFFFFF
- Material specification: PVC or aluminium, UV laminated
- Size: 60 × 40mm (or as required)
- Quantity per entity variant

File naming for badge artwork:
```
CMG-HelmBadge-[Entity]-[YYYY].pdf
```
Example: `CMG-HelmBadge-Engineering-2025.pdf`
