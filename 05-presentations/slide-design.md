# Slide Design Guide

## Software

All branded presentations are built in Microsoft PowerPoint. Do not use Canva, Google Slides, or Keynote for client-facing materials — fonts and layout will not render correctly when converted.

---

## Slide Dimensions

**Widescreen:** 33.87 × 19.05cm (13.33 × 7.5 inches) — 16:9. Use for all presentations.

Do not use 4:3 format. Do not use custom dimensions unless specifically required for a venue display.

---

## Colour Usage in Slides

| Slide Type | Background | Primary Text | Accent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cover / Section divider | Deep Navy (#071E3D) | White | Gold (#C8921A) hairlines |
| Content slide (standard) | White | Text Dark (#0D1B2E) | Brand Blue (#1B4F8A) for headings |
| Accent content slide | Blue Pale (#E8F1FB) | Text Dark | Brand Blue |
| Dark content slide | Blue Mid (#0D3362) | White | Gold hairlines |
| Quote / callout slide | Cream (#F7F4EF) | Text Dark | Gold left border |

---

## Typography in Slides

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Slide title (H1) | Cormorant Garamond | 36–44pt | 400 | White (dark bg) / Text Dark (light bg) |
| Section label / eyebrow | Sora | 9pt | 300, ALL CAPS | Gold (#C8921A) or Text Mid |
| Body / bullet | Noto Sans | 16–20pt | 400 | Text Dark or White |
| Callout / stat | Cormorant Garamond | 48–72pt | 400 | Brand Blue or White |
| Caption | Noto Sans | 11–13pt | 300 | Text Light |
| Table header | Sora | 10pt | 600, ALL CAPS | White on Blue Mid |
| Table body | Noto Sans | 11–13pt | 400 | Text Dark |

**Never use Sora or Noto Sans for slide titles.** Slide titles are H1 — they use Cormorant Garamond.

---

## Layout Grid

Use a 12-column, 4-row implicit grid within the slide. Key margin: **1.4cm** on all sides.

**Content area:** 31.07 × 16.25cm (after margins applied to 33.87 × 19.05cm slide).

Keep body text away from slide edges. Never run text to the edge of a slide.

---

## Logo on Slides

- **Cover slide:** CM lockup (horizontal or stacked, white variant) — top-left or centred, within the safe margin
- **Content slides:** CM mark (small, white) — bottom-right corner, outside the content zone but within the slide. Size: approximately 22pt height.
- **Final / thank you slide:** CM lockup, centred, white, larger

The logo must appear on every slide. It must not be obscured by content.

---

## Gold Hairline Use

The gold hairline (#C8921A, 1.5pt) is used:
- Below the slide title on content slides (horizontal rule, full content width)
- As a left border accent on quote or callout slides (4pt vertical bar)
- As a top accent on the cover slide (full-width rule below the header zone)

Do not use the gold rule as a decorative element throughout — it loses its meaning when overused.

---

## Photography and Images

Site photography and project images are preferred over stock images. When using photography:
- Place images in a consistent position (right panel, full bleed column, or 50% slide coverage)
- Overlay a Navy colour block at 40% opacity if text must appear over the image
- Never stretch or distort images
- No stock photos of generic workers, hard hats, or construction sites that do not represent Citi Maju's actual work

When no photography is available, use a clean colour panel rather than a stock image.

---

## What Not to Do

- No animated transitions (fade is acceptable; wipe, spin, bounce are not)
- No clip art, icon packs, or emoji in client-facing slides
- No WordArt or built-in PowerPoint shapes with gradients or bevels
- No shadow effects on text
- No more than two typefaces per slide (which, following the system, means Garamond for title, Sora or Noto Sans for body)
- No more than five bullet points per slide — split into two slides if needed
- No slides that are more than 70% text — use visuals, tables, or diagrams where possible
