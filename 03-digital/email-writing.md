# Email Writing Guide

## Principles

Every email sent from a Citi Maju address is a brand touchpoint. A poorly written email from a Director carries the same weight as a well-designed brochure — it either builds confidence or erodes it.

Write clearly. Respond to the actual question. Do not pad.

---

## Subject Lines

The subject line is read before the email. Make it specific and useful.

**Format:** `[Type] — [Specific description]`

Examples:
- `Quotation — Pipe Jacking Works, Setia Alam Phase 3`
- `Site Update — Water Reticulation, Bandar Utama (Week 12)`
- `Follow-Up — Meeting on 14 April, Scope Clarification`
- `Submission — Tender Documents, JKR Reference [No.]`

**Do not use:**
- "Hi" or "Hello" as a subject line
- All-caps subject lines
- Vague subjects: "Update", "Question", "Following up"
- Exclamation marks

## Salutations

| Relationship | Salutation |
|---|---|
| First contact, formal | Dear Mr / Ms [Surname], |
| Established relationship, formal | Dear [First name], |
| Group email, formal | Dear Sir / Madam, |
| Ongoing thread, informal | [First name], (no "Dear") |

Use "Dear" for first-contact and formal emails. Once a professional relationship is established and the other party writes informally, matching their register is acceptable.

Do not use: "To whom it may concern" (sounds like a complaint letter), "Hi there", "Hey", "Greetings".

---

## Email Structure

### Opening

State the purpose in the first sentence. Do not use a preamble like "I hope this email finds you well" — go directly to the point.

> "Please find attached our quotation for the water reticulation works at [Project Name], as discussed on [Date]."

> "Following our site visit on [Date], I am writing to confirm the scope of works agreed."

### Body

Use short paragraphs. Separate distinct topics clearly. Use numbered lists for sequential items (next steps, document requirements). Use bullet points for unordered lists (services included, documents attached).

### Closing

End with a clear next action or statement of availability. Do not use filler phrases.

> "Please review and revert at your convenience."
> "I am available for a call this week if you would like to discuss further."
> "Documents have been submitted as requested. Please acknowledge receipt."

### Sign-off

| Context | Sign-off |
|---|---|
| Formal, first contact | Yours sincerely, |
| Established relationship | Best regards, |
| Ongoing thread | Regards, |

Do not use: "Warmly", "Cheers", "Thanks and regards" (mixing two sign-offs), "Yours truly".

---

## Attachments

State in the body what is attached and how many files.

> "Please find attached: (1) Quotation — Ref CME-2025-041; (2) Company profile."

Never send an email with attachments and no body text explaining what the attachments are.

Name files clearly: `CME-Quotation-2025-041-ProjectName.pdf` not `quotation_final_v3_REVISED.pdf`.

---

## Reply Timing and Response Promises

Do not promise a response time. Acknowledge receipt if you cannot respond in full immediately:

> "Thank you for your inquiry. I will revert with the relevant information shortly."

Do not write: "We will respond within 24 hours" or "Our team will contact you within 2 business days."

## Common Email Templates

### Acknowledging a New Inquiry

```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out to Citi Maju Group.

I have noted your inquiry regarding [subject]. I will revert with the relevant details shortly.

If you need to discuss further, you may reach me directly at +6012-379 6562.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Signature]
```

### Sending a Quotation

```
Dear [Name],

Please find attached our quotation for [project description], as requested.

Quotation reference: [Ref No.]
Validity: [X] days from date of issue

Should you have any questions or require clarification on the scope, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Signature]
```

### Following Up After No Response

```
Dear [Name],

I am writing to follow up on my earlier email dated [Date] regarding [subject].

Please let me know if you require any additional information to proceed.

Best regards,
[Name]
[Signature]
```
