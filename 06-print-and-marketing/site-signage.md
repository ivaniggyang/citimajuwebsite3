# Site Signage

## Purpose

Site signage serves two functions: regulatory compliance and brand presence. Both must be done well. A well-branded, properly installed site board tells the client and public that this contractor is serious and professional.

---

## Types of Site Signage

| Type | Description | Size (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Project hoarding board | Large exterior signage on site perimeter or hoarding | 2400 × 1200mm (one panel) |
| Project notice board | Mounted notice at site entrance or office | 900 × 600mm or 1200 × 900mm |
| Safety signage | Hazard, warning, and instruction signs | Various — see safety-signage.md |
| Directional signage | Site access, parking, office directions | 600 × 200mm |
| JKR / authority-mandated board | Regulatory project signboard per authority requirement | As specified by authority |

## Project Hoarding Board

### Required Content
1. Project title and reference
2. Client name (if client has approved)
3. Scope of works (brief — one or two lines)
4. Citi Maju entity: Engineering or Construction
5. CM Lockup (horizontal, appropriate variant)
6. Contact: inquiry@citimaju.com · www.citimaju.com
7. Certifications (Engineering entity only): CIDB G7 · SPAN Registered · Air Selangor Approved
8. Project duration (if appropriate to display publicly)

### Layout

**Background:** Deep Navy (#071E3D), full panel
**Top zone:** CM Horizontal Lockup (white), positioned top-left, 60mm height
**Gold hairline:** 2px rule full panel width, below the logo zone
**Main text zone:**
- Project title: Cormorant Garamond 400, 48–72pt depending on panel size, White
- Scope line: Sora 300, ALL CAPS, 16pt, Text Light
**Bottom zone:**
- Contact details, Sora 300, ALL CAPS, White
- Certification line (Engineering), Sora 300, 12pt, Gold (#C8921A)
**Right panel optional:** Project site photograph or site rendering

## Project Notice Board

For site offices and site entrances. Smaller than a hoarding board but carries the same brand standards.

### Required Content
Same as hoarding board, minus the large photographic element. Add:
- Site supervisor name and contact
- Start date and target completion date
- Emergency contact number

### Format
- **Background:** Warm Paper or White (for readability)
- **Header block:** Navy (#071E3D) background, CM lockup white, full width header
- **Content:** Text Dark on white/warm paper, structured table layout
- **Border:** 2px Navy border, outer edge of the board
- **Laminate:** Weatherproof laminate or mounted on aluminium composite panel (ACP)

## Authority-Mandated Signboards

Certain projects (JKR, local authority, Air Selangor) require a specific signboard format as a condition of permit or contract. In these cases:
- Follow the authority's format for the mandatory fields
- Apply Citi Maju branding within the constraints permitted
- If a logo placement zone is provided, use the appropriate CM Lockup
- Do not modify the required regulatory information

Contact the project manager for the specific signboard requirements for each project before fabricating.

## Materials and Fabrication

| Application | Material | Finish |
|---|---|---|
| Hoarding board (exterior) | 3mm aluminium composite panel (ACP) | UV-resistant digital print, gloss or matte |
| Notice board | 2mm ACP or 6mm PVC foam board | Digital print, gloss laminate |
| Temporary site signs | Corflute / polypropylene sheet | Digital print |
| Permanent entry sign | Stainless steel or brushed aluminium | Etched or screen-printed |

For all exterior applications: specify UV-stable inks. Standard digital prints without UV protection will fade within six months in Malaysian conditions.

---

## Typography on Site Signage

At large scale (hoarding), type sizes are translated from the design file. Key principle: text must be readable from the furthest practical viewing distance.

Minimum font size on a 2400 × 1200mm board: 24pt in the design file (approximately 40mm on the physical board).

Use the same font hierarchy as all other materials. Do not introduce alternative fonts on signage because "it looks bolder" — use Sora 700 or 800 for maximum weight if bold signage is needed.

## Compliance Note

Site signage in Malaysia may be subject to local authority requirements (MBPJ, MBSA, MPSJ, DBKL, etc.) and construction contract specifications. Always confirm signboard requirements with the client and the relevant authority before fabrication. Produce unapproved signboards only at your own risk of replacement cost.
