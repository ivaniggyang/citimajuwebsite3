# Vehicle Livery

## Purpose

Company vehicles are mobile brand touchpoints. A site lorry, water tanker, or supervisor's pickup seen across a project site reinforces that Citi Maju is organised, professional, and present. An unbranded or poorly branded vehicle does the opposite.

---

## Vehicle Types

| Type | Livery Requirement |
|---|---|
| Pickup trucks / SUVs (supervisor vehicles) | Full livery |
| Lorries / tipper trucks | Side and rear panel livery |
| Excavators and heavy equipment | Decal placement, no full wrap |
| Rental equipment (not owned) | No livery — do not brand equipment you do not own |

---

## Livery Design

### Primary Colour Scheme

**Option A — Dark:**
- Main body panels: Deep Navy (#071E3D)
- Accent stripe: Refined Gold (#C8921A), 40–50mm horizontal stripe along the lower body panel
- Logo: CM Lockup (horizontal, white variant) — driver's door and passenger's door, centred on the door panel

**Option B — Light (for visibility/safety requirements):**
- Main panels: White or Silver (vehicle's base colour retained)
- Navy block on door panel background behind the logo
- Gold stripe accent along lower body

Use Option A for management and supervisor vehicles. Use Option B if the fleet operates in high-traffic or safety-sensitive areas where high-visibility is required.

---

## Logo Placement

**Driver's door and passenger's door:**
- CM Horizontal Lockup (white on navy panel)
- Height: 120–160mm depending on door size
- Entity line below: "CITI MAJU ENGINEERING" or "CITI MAJU CONSTRUCTION" as applicable
- Centred vertically and horizontally on the door panel

**Rear (pickup tail gate or lorry rear panel):**
- CM Mark only (white or navy depending on background)
- Height: 200mm
- Centred

**Vehicle side (lorries and long-panel vehicles):**
- CM Lockup (horizontal, white or navy as appropriate to background)
- Contact line below: `www.citimaju.com · inquiry@citimaju.com`
- Sora 300, ALL CAPS, 24pt in design file (approximately 40mm on vehicle)

---

## Certification Line (Engineering Vehicles)

For Citi Maju Engineering vehicles, add below the logo on the door panel:
`CIDB G7 · SPAN REGISTERED · AIR SELANGOR APPROVED`
Sora 300, ALL CAPS, small — approximately 18–20mm on vehicle.

Do not add certification text on Construction entity vehicles.

---

## Decals vs Vinyl Wrap

| Method | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cut vinyl decals | Logos and text on existing paint | Most cost-effective, clean finish |
| Printed vinyl wrap | Full colour panels or complex graphics | More expensive; full wrap not required for this brand |
| Magnetic signs | Temporary or multi-use vehicles | Acceptable for short-term use only — not preferred |

For permanent fleet vehicles, use cut vinyl or printed vinyl panel sections, not magnetic signs.

---

## Typography on Vehicles

Body copy is unnecessary on vehicles — only the logo, entity name, certifications, and contact URL. All text elements should be drawn from the brand master files, not retyped.

Font sizes (in vehicle-actual dimensions, not design file points):
- Company name: 50–80mm height
- Entity line: 20–25mm height
- Contact URL: 35–40mm height

---

## Colour Specifications for Vinyl

When ordering vinyl from a signage vendor, provide the following colour references:

| Colour | HEX | Vinyl Equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | #071E3D | Request colour match — approximately Avery Dennison 805 Dark Blue or equivalent |
| Refined Gold | #C8921A | Request colour match — approximately Avery Dennison 853 Gold Metallic or equivalent |
| White | #FFFFFF | Standard white |

Request a colour test strip before applying to vehicles. Navy and gold vinyl colours vary significantly across brands.

---

## Approval Before Application

All livery designs must be approved by the Director before application to any vehicle. Provide a design mock-up on a vehicle silhouette (side view, front, rear) as a PDF for review. Do not apply livery without written approval.
